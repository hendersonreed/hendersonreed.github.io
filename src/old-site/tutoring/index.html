What This Is

This is a prototype tool for tutoring, allowing users to enter a session with a video conferencing call and a collaborative whiteboard together.

Simply enter a name in the above text input and hit "start session" You'll be redirected to a page where a whiteboard and video call are running. Make sure you allow microphone and camera access (this is to allow the iframe running the video call to have access to those.)

Note: these aren't password protected, so the length of the room name is directly proportional to how likely it is that someone could stumble into your call. As a result, consider making your room name at least 10 characters, or as many as 20 for added entropy
