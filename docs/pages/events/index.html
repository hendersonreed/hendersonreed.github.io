SUDO events

The Society for Unrealized Digital Occurrences (SUDO) is a loose organization of programmers, artists, thinkers, and enthusiasts working on a variety of topics, operating in Portland, Oregon.

Currently, it’s comprised of two regularly occurring events:

  • the HCIANMDG is a discussion group about humans, computers, and how we interact with them. This gets pretty deep into the philosophical weeds - more end-user programming and digital culture, less Fitt’s law and UI frameworks.
  • the CCN is a regularly-occurring informal hackathon. Get together, program something new for 2 hours, and (optionally) demo to your peers at the end.
